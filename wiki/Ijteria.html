Ijteria

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ijteria is a country located on Andersia, in Northern Leskomia. It shares land borders with Austeria
to the east, ???1 to the south-east, Rumia to the south, Ferland to the west and ???2 to the
north-west. Its capital and largest city is Beravja.

Inhabited since at least the [name] Era, it is populated predominantly by the Andersian Ijters,
(%98.2) as well as Rumians, Fers (%1.6) and Humans (%0.2), the latter of which are
chiefly research personel.

Culture

While Leskomia saw less migrations and less contact between populations as would be expected of
most Human nations, the continuous presence of the Andersian Ijters in the territory of
present-day Ijteria for millenia has allowed the country to benefit from a deep and extraordinarily
rich culture.
